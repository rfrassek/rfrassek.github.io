Synopsis

The GATIS Network and Durham University are organising a one-week school on integrabilty held in Durham from 6th to 10th July 2015. The school is intended as a training for advanced Master's students and doctoral students with an interest in integrability techniques. In order to create a pedagogical and informal atmosphere during the school, most lectures are designed and presented by young scientists with experience in the field. To foster interactions between attendees and lecturers, accommodation and meals will be provided in the nearby Grey College.

Applications are now being accepted.
By applying you commit to attend the whole school and to pay the gross amount of £420. This fee includes your accommodation (en-suite room in Grey College), all meals (breakfast, lunch and dinner) for the duration of the school as well as social activities. Please apply before 19th April; late applications may not receive full consideration. Due to logistic constraints we cannot guarantee that all applicants will be accepted. We will inform you later in April on whether your application has been accepted. At that time you will be requested to pay the school fee and you may plan your trip to Durham. If you have read all of the above, you may proceed to the application form. Please do not hesitate to contact us if you have further questions.
