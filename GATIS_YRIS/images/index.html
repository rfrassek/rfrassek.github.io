Synopsis

The GATIS Network and Durham University are organising a one-week school on integrabilty held in Durham from 6th to 10th July 2015. The school is intended as a training for advanced Master's students and doctoral students with an interest in integrability techniques. In order to create a pedagogical and informal atmosphere during the school, most lectures are designed and presented by young scientists with experience in the field. To foster interactions between attendees and lecturers, accommo- dation and meals will be provided in a nearby college.
