Modena, 3. - 5. June 2025

Workshop on integrable systems

Date: June 3rd - June 5th, 2025

The goal of the workshop is to gather researchers from the Po Valley, their nearest and next-to-nearest neighbors, in order to foster discussions on topics related to integrable systems.

The topics include: String and gauge theories, Probability, Quantum phyisics, Statistical physics, Condensed matter, Quantum groups and Representation theory.

Mini-courses by:

  • Cristian Giardinà (tbc) tba
  • Lorenzo Piroli Exactly solvable models of random quantum circuits

Code of conduct

The meeting is dedicated to providing a welcoming and inclusive environment for all participants. We are committed to ensure that everyone feels respected, valued and included, regardless of their role or experience. We also ask to give credit when using the work or ideas of others and to respect the confidentiality of unpublished results.
