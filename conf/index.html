Workshop on integrable systems

Date: June 3rd - June 5th

The goal of the workshop is to gather researchers in the po valley their nearest and next-to-nearest neighbors to foster discussions on topics related to integrable systems.

The topics include: String and gauge theories, Probability, Quantum phyisics, Statistical physics, Quantum groups and Representation theory.

Organisation committee

  • Andrea Cavaglià (University of Turin)
  • Rouven Frassek (University of Modena and Reggio Emilia)
  • Chiara Paletta (University of Ljubljana)
  • Andrea Trombettoni (University of Trieste)
The workshop is supported by the FAR UNIMORE project CUP-E93C23002040005.

Code of conduct

The meeting is dedicated to providing a welcoming and inclusive environment for all participants. We are committed to ensure that everyone feels respected, valued and included, regardless of their role or experience.

It is important to be aware of and respect intellectual property. The following principles should be adhered to:

  • Attribution: Always give credit when using the work or ideas of others.
  • Originality: Ensure that your contributions are original and do not infringe upon the intellectual property of others.
  • Confidentiality: Respect the confidentiality of any disclosed information.

Any form of discrimination, harassment or unethical influence will not be tolerated.
